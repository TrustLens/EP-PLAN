Policyc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/ep-plan#Policy

A generic representation of a collection of rules that may be applicable in the context of a specific plan. A policy can provide a rationale for constraints being applied to steps as well as explain reasons including other plan elements. For example, a data protection policy might require that steps are executed by specific agents and that a data anonymisation step is included in the plan.
is in domain of
isPolicyOfPlan op
is in range of
includesPolicy op

Rationalec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/ep-plan#Rationale

A generic concept describing a specific justification of a plan or an individual plan element. For example, a data anonymisation step might be included in the plan to protect personal information as a result of a data protection policy associated with a plan (i.e. the reason for including such a step in a plan). Similarly, a data protection policy associated with a plan can be linked to a description of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) which provides the reason why such policy was implemented.
is in domain of
isRationaleOf op
is in range of
hasRationale op
